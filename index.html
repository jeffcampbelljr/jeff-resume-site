I listened to what customers were asking for — better integrations, smoother handoffs, and real tech relationships. That’s when I realized: partnerships weren’t just helpful — they were *strategic*.

The Climb

I built out a custom Salesforce system with full automation. I drove direct sales. I landed a partnership that brought over 9,000 stores to the platform. The Operator
